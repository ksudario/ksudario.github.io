About

Hello there! My name is Katrina

I was born and raised in the Philippines. I grew up in the city with all the traffic, huge malls and skyscrapers but I have spent all of my adult years living in the country from Hawaii's North Shore to a small college town in Arkansas. I have a six years old son who loves Disney classics, theme parks and the local fair.


I was 17 years old when I first enrolled in college, I had hopes of becoming a Diplomat so I took a major in International Studies with a focus on International Politics but life had a different path for me and I got a scholarship to go to school in Hawaii! Who would want to pass on that opportunity, right? So at 19 years old, I flew to Hawaii and attended Brigham Young University-Hawaii. I graduated in 2012 with a degree in International Cultural Studies.


I obviously didn't become a diplomat. Life is full of surprises, I ended up finding a platform where I can offer my services while staying home with my son. This platform has helped me land clients who later on became my mentors. I have done a lot of work in the Digital Marketing space and I have helped a number of online entrepreneurs and personalities build their brand and launch their businesses. And now, during the craziest year we all had, I am embarking on a new journey as a Software Engineer!
